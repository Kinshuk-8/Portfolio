MedWeb

MedWeb connects local medical shops on a unified digital platform, offering real-time inventory, easy search, and delivery options for quick and reliable access to medicines and healthcare essentials.

Live Link

Project Overview

MedWeb is a comprehensive digital platform designed to connect local medical shops, bringing them together on a single, accessible network. It offers real-time inventory tracking, allowing users to easily search for medicines, healthcare essentials, and medical supplies available at nearby stores. With an intuitive interface, MedWeb ensures a seamless experience, enabling customers to locate, compare, and purchase the required products without unnecessary delays.

Additionally, the platform supports delivery services, ensuring medicines reach customers quickly and reliably. By bridging the gap between pharmacies and consumers, MedWeb enhances accessibility, reduces medicine shortages, and minimizes the hassle of physically visiting multiple stores. This solution ensures efficiency, convenience, and reliability in healthcare shopping.
